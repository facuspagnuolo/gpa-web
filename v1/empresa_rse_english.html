CSR, Corporate Social Responsibility Policies

COMMITMENT TO THE COMMUNITY

Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts promotes the improvement of the community’s quality of life. Our solidarity actions meet the social situation in which we are immersed and reflect our intention to help people in need. For these reasons, we make community donations mainly focused on education, contribute to improving public health conditions and help non-governmental organizations.
Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts’s community actions are performed in the following areas:

EDUCATION: Donations for building refurbishment. Teaching Material and other resources to improve the schools’ educational potential and sport practices.

HEALTH: Donations for buying equipment and improving infrastructures.

COMMUNITY: Donations to volunteer firefighters, C.A.N.D.A (Organization for differently-abled people), and Parochial Caritas, a soup kitchen for almost 50 unprivileged children.

PRESERVE THE ENVIRONMENT

Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts commits itself to preserve the environment. Our commitment is to protect the environment through actions and measures oriented towards prevention of any kind of contamination that could arise from our work or our suppliers'.

• We prioritize environmental safety, health and protection as foundations for our improvement.

• We develop safe habits and best practices among our personnel. Some of our environmental principles are: environmental protection, proper handling of waste materials and effective energy use.

• Some of our internal environmental practices are: paper, toner, battery and plastic recycling.

• We spot ways for reducing energy consumption.

• We adopt practices for the handling of waste materials and have the required infrastructure to classify the elements according to their origin so as to be able to recycle them.

• We train our personnel through talks on environmental best practices, encouraging them to adopt healthy actions.
