Our Principles

• We commit ourselves to understanding the needs and requirements of all our customer/suppliers, trying to meet them while maintaining Golden's business objectives.
• We continually improve the quality of our products and services through the knowledge of our customers’ requirements and the unrelenting dedication to improve our processes.
• We make profits by complying with the commitments made to our customers, using highly effective business processes and a proper risk management.
• We maximize employee participation through proper communication and training.
• We make decisions regarding our short-term risks and potential profits with long-term success in mind.
• We value experience and knowledge, using initiative, creativity and dedication as means to reach our goals.
• We use honest and objective evaluations of our performance, comparing our work against our goals for profit and customer/supplier satisfaction.
