Management System

Our processes are carried out under a solid Quality Management System, certified by the ISO 9001:2008 standard. The safety of our products is ensured throughout the process with an HACCP system, meticulously developed to take into account the special features of the raw material and certified by an external organization of international renown.

In addition, we implement a Safety and Health System which allows us to maintain the welfare of our main strength: our people.


Quality Policies and Nourishing Security

The commitment of Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts team is to develop, produce peanut and provide services to meet all our customers' requirements in accordance with our principles.

We select and assist our suppliers so that they provide us with products and services that enable us to fulfill our commitment.

Our Company will provide the resources and necessary training so that every member of the Company may contribute through continuous improvement to consistently achieve a high standard of quality.

Safety Policies

Golden Peanut and Tree Nuts considers their employees its most valuable asset, healthy and safe working conditions will be a first priority and will receive support and participation from all levels.

The Company defines as its prevention goal to develop and maintain working activities under appropriate Safe and Hygienic conditions.

From this policy it arises that:
• Accidents can and must be prevented.
• Accident causes can be eliminated or controlled.
• All permanent and temporary personnel share and irrevocable social obligation towards work accident prevention, which, in addition, becomes an employment requirement.
