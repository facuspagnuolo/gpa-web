Peanuts properties

• Peanuts contain high amounts of antioxidants.
• They contain proteins and monounsaturated fats which reduce blood cholesterol.
• They contain vitamins such as: E,B1, B2, B3 and B6
• They provide minerals such as: potassium, sodium, calcium, magnesium, copper, fluoride, zinc, and selenium.
• Since they contain oleic-linoleic acid, they benefit the cardiovascular system and help the nervous system function correctly.
• Peanuts are very rich in phosphorus and iron. For this reason, it is recommended for anemic people and pregnant women.
